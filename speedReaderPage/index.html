speedReader

Github:

This is perfect if you want to read ahead in a topic or want to skim an article online. This can help you get your work done faster and quicker!
This project is based off Rapid Serial Visual Presentation.
TL;DR: If you don't have to move your eyes to read different words, your reading speed increases drastically

It currently allows you to speed read text from your clipboard, but I am working to add epub and pdf support soon
